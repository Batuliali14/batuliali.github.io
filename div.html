Cities in Kenya

Mombasa

This is a popular destination because of its beautiful beaches.The swahili slang associated with it is 'Mombasa raha'.It has really amazimg people with captivating cultures.

Nairobi

This is the capital city of Kenya.It hosts so many international organisations.Its known as the place of cool waters.It has so many buildings and skyscrapers.

Kisumu

Its popular because of having lake victoria.The lake has so many varieties of fish.Its one of the major cities in the Nyanza region.

Nakuru

Its another region that was uplifted to be a city recently.It has beautiful Flamingoes.The nature of the place is also very appealing.
