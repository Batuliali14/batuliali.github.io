High School Experience.

With my perspective my journey began when i started High School.
I joined High School on 2019,I was 13 years old...i was Young right?
After seeing my Parents struggle and how much they believed in me i chose to change.
I literally started valuing studies and got supports from my teachers.
My English teacher was my role model.
At times her advises made me cry but Thanks to her.
My KSCE went good and become the top rank student.
Am grateful Alhamdulillah.

Life After Form 4.

I thought form 4 was the end of studies.My expectations were different from the reality.
On January i started Computer classes;I never stayed at home after form 4.Apparently things weren't going as i expected.
Life started getting harder and harder.I never stopped crying due to family issues.I acted strong infront of my younger siblings.Am not a don't care person small things hurt.
I had a dream of studing outside Kenya,I even got scholarships at Sudan and Malaysia but sadly my mom disagreed.At that time I started giving up but then thought of a phrase that says"What's yours will always remain yours!".
Alhamdulillah I joined TUM and right now am at pwaniteknowgalz and Swahilipot where am mentored.
It's a great opportunity indeed.
